  • Description

    • Every dragon, every variant, every portrait, tokenized and ready for use!

    A comprehensive collection of portrait and token artwork for almost 430 dragons, including subtypes and variants! This all-new premium module, exclusive to Foundry VTT, assembles all of the dragon artwork from multiple books, including the Draconic Codex for Pathfinder Second Edition Remastered, into a complete set of tokens and portraits ready to be dropped into your games, featuring every member of the time-honored chromatic and metallic families alongside newcomers like the duplicitous conspirator dragon, the malevolent diabolic dragon, and the ruinous despair dragon, as well as other draconic monstrosities like the insatiable ravener and the vengeful wyrmwraith. Challenge even the most battle-hardened heroes with some of the most legendary creatures to walk the face of Golarion!

    This unique, exclusive bundle includes:

    • Tokens representing every variant and age category for each of the 50+ remastered and pre-remaster dragons appearing across the Bestiary, Bestiary 2, Bestiary 3, Monster Core, Monster Core 2, and Draconic Codex compendiums included with the Pathfinder Second Edition game system for Foundry VTT, as well as alternative portraits and illustrations for dragon NPCs sourced from Paizo's vast vault of artwork!*
    • Full support for the Pathfinder Anachronism module for the Starfinder Second Edition game system, allowing you to bring these draconic terrors into your Starfinder games!
    • Full support for Foundry VTT’s Dynamic Token Engine, granting access to a wide variety of token ring options and enabling the appearance of tokens to be modified in real time as they take damage or are afflicted with conditions!
    • Full compatibility with the Pathfinder Tokens: Character Gallery module, automatically adding every included token and portrait to your existing library of NPC art!
    • A unique new Dynamic Token Ring exclusive to this module, including a blank copy that can be used to create matching tokens for your own homebrew creatures and player characters!

     

    *  Some art has previously appeared in other modules, such as the standalone Bestiaries and Monster Core token packs. Certain dragon variants, such as spellcasters and age categories not yet illustrated, share the same art.

     

    The Foundry Virtual Tabletop software (version 13 or higher) is required to use this product. For more information, visit https://foundryvtt.com/.

    Installation

    Purchasing this product from the Paizo store will grant you an activation code which can be used through your Foundry VTT account page to activate the content for installation. Afterwards you will be able to install the “Pathfinder Token Pack: Draconic Codex" module from the Foundry Virtual Tabletop setup screen.

    After installing the module, it must be activated within a Game World. To do so:

    • Access the Settings Sidebar tab
    • Click "Manage Modules"
    • Locate the "Pathfinder Token Pack: Draconic Codex" Module, enable it, and save module settings.

    Once enabled, the Pathfinder Token Pack: Draconic Codex module automatically maps portrait and token art to every creature in the Draconic Codex compendium pack included with your Pathfinder Second Edition game system. Tokens and portraits will be automatically displayed when you open either the compendium browser or the compendium packs directly.
