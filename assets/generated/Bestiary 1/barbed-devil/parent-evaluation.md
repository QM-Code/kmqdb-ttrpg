Process guide: `docs/IMAGE_GENERATION_WORKFLOW.md`

Reference target: Pathfinder RPG Bestiary 1 page 72 Barbed Devil. The image direction uses the Pathfinder devil reference rather than generic demon, armored knight, or insect monster art.

Core Pathfinder traits: humanoid devil covered in jagged barbs and hooked spines, lean predatory posture, clawed hands, spined tail or back silhouette, infernal menace, and disciplined devil anatomy. Avoid balor wings, heavy armor, insect-only body, skeleton drift, and generic horned demon proportions.
