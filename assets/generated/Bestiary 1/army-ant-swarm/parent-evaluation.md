Process guide: `docs/IMAGE_GENERATION_WORKFLOW.md`

## Checklist Evaluation

- Silhouette match: lanes 2 and 4 should best communicate a swarm mass rather than a single creature.
- Anatomy match: all lanes target many ants with repeated small bodies, legs, and mandibles.
- Avoids wrong interpretations: prompts avoid single giant ant, humanoid ant soldier, wings, and emblem-like designs.
- Creature-first read: the swarm should feel like an active hazard and enemy mass.
- Tabletop/CRPG usefulness: two light and two dark variants are present.

Use `army-ant-swarm-silhouette-light-surging-carpet.png` for UI readability and `army-ant-swarm-crpg-dark-devouring-wave.png` for encounter art. If refining, keep the creature as a swarm rather than letting one foreground ant dominate.
