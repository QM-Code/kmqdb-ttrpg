# Balor Four-Lane Parent Evaluation

Process guide: `docs/IMAGE_GENERATION_WORKFLOW.md`

Reference target: Pathfinder RPG Bestiary 1 page 58 Balor. The image direction uses the Pathfinder demon reference rather than generic horned devil or dragon art.

Core Pathfinder traits: towering winged demon general, flaming whip, sword or heavy weapon presence, horned head, massive muscular body, fiery aura, vast bat-like wings, and apocalyptic battlefield posture. Avoid dragon-only forms, armored knight drift, small imp proportions, and non-demonic angel cues.

## Checklist Evaluation

- Silhouette match: lanes 2 and 4 should best show horns, wings, and whip.
- Anatomy match: all lanes target a towering winged demon rather than a dragon or devil knight.
- Avoids wrong interpretations: prompts avoid dragon-only, angel, imp, and armored mortal drift.
- Creature-first read: all lanes should read as a major demon commander.
- Tabletop/CRPG usefulness: two light and two dark variants are present.
