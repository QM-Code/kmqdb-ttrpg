# Animated Object Prompts and Lane Notes

Reference: `pathfinder-bestiary-1-page-14-reference.webp`

Shared Pathfinder constraints:
- Animated cage construct containing a rattling dead skeleton.
- Metal and wood domed cage body with curved ribs/bars and spikes.
- Chain legs and chain arms ending in claw-like hooks.
- Lurching construct posture.
- Avoid living flesh monster, robot, simple empty cage, humanoid wearing cage armor, normal person inside, sci-fi machinery, gore, text, and watermark.

## Lane 1: Bestiary Light

Output: `animated-object-bestiary-light-cage-skeleton.png`

Prompt summary: Pathfinder-style painterly bestiary illustration on a light parchment/neutral background. Full creature in frame, three-quarter view, aged greenish metal and worn wood cage, sharp spikes, round base, visible skeleton inside, chain limbs with hook pincers.

Lane notes:
- Strongest classic bestiary read; the cage body is the creature and the skeleton is clearly trapped inside.
- Light parchment background supports site use on bright surfaces.
- Keeps all major anatomy visible with generous padding.

## Lane 2: Silhouette Light

Output: `animated-object-silhouette-light-chain-hooks.png`

Prompt summary: Light-background game enemy silhouette with clean readable contour, centered full body, one raised chain arm, hook legs stepping forward, spiked cage and skeleton visible through bars.

Lane notes:
- Best thumbnail silhouette of the light variants because the cage is tilted and chain hooks spread outward.
- Reads as a mobile prison-object rather than a humanoid costume.
- Slightly more mechanical polish in joints, but still chain-and-hook construct rather than robot.

## Lane 3: Bestiary Dark

Output: `animated-object-bestiary-dark-spiked-cage.png`

Prompt summary: Pathfinder-style painterly bestiary illustration on a dark charcoal-brown background with cool rim light, dust/smoke, full cage construct, visible skeleton, spiked metal-and-wood ribs, chain limbs and hook claws.

Lane notes:
- Best dark-theme production asset for static bestiary display.
- The dark backdrop separates the cage bars, spikes, chains, and skeleton clearly.
- Maintains the source creature's core anatomy without adding flesh or unrelated monster features.

## Lane 4: CRPG Dark

Output: `animated-object-crpg-dark-lurching-cage.png`

Prompt summary: Dynamic CRPG combat/bestiary asset with the animated cage lunging forward on chain legs, chain arms whipping outward, skeleton rattling inside, dramatic dark smoky backdrop, cool rim light and warm metal glints.

Lane notes:
- Most action-oriented lane; useful for encounter cards, combat UI, or dramatic monster presentation.
- Close camera and diagonal motion make the construct feel aggressive while preserving the cage-first identity.
- Hook claws are near the frame edges but remain visible.
