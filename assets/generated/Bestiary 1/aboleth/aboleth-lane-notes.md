# Aboleth Lane Notes

Reference: `pathfinder-bestiary-1-page-8-reference.webp`

Shared hard constraints: huge ancient aquatic aberration; elongated fish-like body; three red eyes; broad red mouth; four long writhing flank tentacles; slick green-gray slime; dark blue-black fins and spines; no limbs; avoid dragon, cthulhu, squid, or octopus-only drift.
