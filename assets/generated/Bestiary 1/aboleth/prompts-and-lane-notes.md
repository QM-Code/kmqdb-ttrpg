# Aboleth Prompts and Lane Notes

Process guide: `docs/IMAGE_GENERATION_WORKFLOW.md`

Reference source: Pathfinder RPG Bestiary 1 page 8, saved as `pathfinder-bestiary-1-page-8-reference.webp`.

Core Pathfinder traits extracted from the page: huge ancient aquatic aberration; elongated fish-like body; three red eyes clustered on the face; broad red mouth; four long writhing flank tentacles; slick green-gray body glistening with clear slime; dark blue-black fins and spines; no limbs. Avoid humanoid torso, dragon wings, Cthulhu-face drift, octopus-only body, and cropped tentacles.

## Lane 1: Painterly/Bestiary Light

Image: `aboleth-bestiary-light-three-eyed-slime.png`

Prompt summary: full-body Aboleth on a pale underwater parchment-gray background, with cold aquatic light, red face details, dark fins, green-gray slime, and all tentacles visible.

Judging notes: primary reference-faithful light lane. It should preserve the long fish body and flank-tentacle arrangement rather than becoming a squid or eel.

## Lane 2: Readable Silhouette Light

Image: `aboleth-silhouette-light-writhing-tentacles.png`

Prompt summary: side/three-quarter swimming pose on a light foggy underwater backdrop, emphasizing separated tentacles and a clear huge-aberration silhouette.

Judging notes: strongest intended thumbnail lane. It should be checked for exactly four main tentacles and a fish-like body.

## Lane 3: Painterly/Bestiary Dark

Image: `aboleth-bestiary-dark-abyssal-slime.png`

Prompt summary: full-body Aboleth in dark deep-sea smoky water, with slime highlights and eerie cold rim light.

Judging notes: dark-theme bestiary counterpart, preserving the same anatomy while making the red eyes and mouth readable.

## Lane 4: Dynamic CRPG Dark

Image: `aboleth-crpg-dark-ancient-lunge.png`

Prompt summary: Aboleth lunging/swimming forward in a dark deep-sea encounter space, tentacles reaching outward but contained in frame.

Judging notes: most encounter-ready variant. It should still read as the Pathfinder Aboleth, not a generic kraken or tentacled horror.
