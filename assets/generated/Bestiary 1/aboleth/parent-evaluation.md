Process guide: `docs/IMAGE_GENERATION_WORKFLOW.md`

Reference target: Pathfinder RPG Bestiary 1 page 8 Aboleth. The image direction uses the Pathfinder page as the source rather than general sea monster or tentacled horror art.

Core Pathfinder traits: huge elongated fish-like aberration, three red eyes, broad red mouth, four long flank tentacles, slick green-gray slime, dark fins/spines, and no limbs.

## Checklist Evaluation

- Silhouette match: lanes 2 and 4 should be strongest because the tentacles are emphasized.
- Anatomy match: all prompts require fish-like body, three eyes, red mouth, and four flank tentacles.
- Avoids wrong interpretations: prompts exclude humanoid torso, dragon wings, Cthulhu-face drift, and octopus-only body.
- Creature-first read: all lanes target an ancient aquatic aberration rather than an ordinary animal.
- Tabletop/CRPG usefulness: two light and two dark variants are present for theme flexibility.

## Parent Recommendation

Use `aboleth-bestiary-light-three-eyed-slime.png` for the clean reference-faithful light asset and `aboleth-crpg-dark-ancient-lunge.png` for encounter presentation if visual QA confirms the tentacles remain contained and readable.

## Suggested Next Correction

If refining, compare against the saved reference page and correct any lane that loses the three-eye face, adds limbs, or becomes too octopus-like. Keep the long fish body and four long side tentacles as the main silhouette.
