# Aasimar Prompts and Lane Notes

Process guide: `docs/IMAGE_GENERATION_WORKFLOW.md`

Reference source: Pathfinder RPG Bestiary 1 page 7, saved as `pathfinder-bestiary-1-page-7-reference.webp`.

Core Pathfinder traits extracted from the page: supernatural but mostly human celestial outsider; calm benevolent presence; short metallic blond hair; pale luminous skin; white flowing robe with gold trim and celestial clasp; slim ceremonial staff or mace-like rod; bare feet; daylight aura. Avoid giant wings, demonic cues, modern clothing, heavy armor, seductive fashion framing, and photorealism.

## Lane 1: Painterly/Bestiary Light

Image: `aasimar-bestiary-light-celestial-cleric.png`

Prompt summary: full-body celestial outsider cleric on a clean warm parchment-white light background; polished painterly bestiary illustration; white, pale gold, ivory, and faint celestial blue palette; calm daylight halo.

Judging notes: strongest direct bestiary read and clearest reference alignment. The light background preserves robe detail, staff silhouette, and benevolent celestial mood.

## Lane 2: Readable Silhouette Light

Image: `aasimar-silhouette-light-daylit-outsider.png`

Prompt summary: full-body Aasimar with a simplified readable contour; staff separated from the body; pale cream-gray background; bright rim light and subtle gold aura.

Judging notes: intended for thumbnail readability and clean UI placement. Watch for over-simplification of the staff/robe relationship when reviewing visually.

## Lane 3: Painterly/Bestiary Dark

Image: `aasimar-bestiary-dark-celestial-radiance.png`

Prompt summary: the same Pathfinder Aasimar traits against a deep blue-black smoky void; warm celestial rim light; serene sacred mood.

Judging notes: best dark-theme bestiary counterpart if the figure remains calm and luminous rather than turning demonic or angelic-winged.

## Lane 4: Dynamic CRPG Dark

Image: `aasimar-crpg-dark-daylight-herald.png`

Prompt summary: full-body celestial cleric stepping forward with staff angled clearly; dark smoky encounter backdrop; gold-white radiance cutting through blue smoke.

Judging notes: most encounter-ready lane. It should remain an Aasimar cleric/NPC creature asset, not a generic winged angel or armored paladin.
