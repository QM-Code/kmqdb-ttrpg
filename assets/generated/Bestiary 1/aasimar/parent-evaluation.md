## Checklist Evaluation

- Silhouette match: Lane 2 should be strongest; lanes 3 and 4 should remain readable if staff and robe are separated from the background.
- Anatomy match: all lanes target mostly human celestial outsider anatomy rather than monstrous or winged angel anatomy.
- Avoids wrong interpretations: prompts explicitly avoid giant wings, demonic cues, modern fashion, heavy armor, and seductive romance-cover framing.
- Creature-first read: this is a humanoid outsider/NPC entry, so the goal is supernatural bestiary presence rather than animalistic monster design.
- Tabletop/CRPG usefulness: two light and two dark variants are present for website theme flexibility.

## Parent Recommendation

Use `aasimar-bestiary-light-celestial-cleric.png` as the primary light-theme reference-faithful asset and `aasimar-bestiary-dark-celestial-radiance.png` as the primary dark-theme companion. Use `aasimar-crpg-dark-daylight-herald.png` where a more active encounter pose is useful.

If refining, compare all four images against the saved page and correct any drift toward winged angel, fashion model, heavy-armored paladin, or demonic celestial. Keep the staff, white robe, bare feet, metallic blond hair, and calm daylight aura.
