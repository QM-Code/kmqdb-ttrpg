# Bat Swarm Four-Lane Parent Evaluation

Process guide: `docs/IMAGE_GENERATION_WORKFLOW.md`

Reference target: Pathfinder RPG Bestiary 1 page 30 Bat Swarm. The image direction uses the Pathfinder bat swarm entry rather than a single giant bat or vampire-themed monster.

Core Pathfinder traits: mass of many small bats, chaotic cloud movement, leathery wings, tiny mammal bodies, red or dark eyes only as subtle accents, and swarm behavior. Avoid one oversized central bat dominating the scene, humanoid vampire cues, and bird flock drift.
