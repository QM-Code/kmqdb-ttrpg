# Basidirond Four-Lane Parent Evaluation

Process guide: `docs/IMAGE_GENERATION_WORKFLOW.md`

Reference target: Pathfinder RPG Bestiary 1 page 28 Basidirond. The image direction uses the Pathfinder fungal creature reference rather than generic carnivorous plant, mushroom forest, or humanoid plant art.

Core Pathfinder traits: strange fungal monster, bell/cap-like upper body, spore-bearing growths, rootlike or tendril-like lower anatomy, alien plant/fungus silhouette, and toxic hallucination-spore mood. Avoid humanoid treant anatomy, ordinary flower monster, sea anemone drift, and mushroom scenery without a creature body.
